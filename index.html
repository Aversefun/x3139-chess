Modes:

  • Default - Can only move tiles with one of your pieces on, either move a piece or move a tile each turn
  • Multi - Can only move tiles with one of your pieces on, move a piece and move a tile each turn
  • Omni - Can move any tile, either move a piece or move a tile each turn
  • Chaos - Can move any tile, move a piece and move a tile each turn
